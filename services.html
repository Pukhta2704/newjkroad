Services

We let out different types of automobiles ranging from trucks to buses and light motor vehicles.

  • Election Commission for every election held in J&K since 1996.
  • Organisations and entities involved in State Security.
  • Jammu and Kashmir State Road Transport Corporation.
  • Picnics and excursions for employees of corporate entities.
  • Businesses for goods and materials movement.
  • Tour service providers including private tourists and Jammu & Kashmir Tourism Development Corporation.
